Flower Shop Catalog

Project intro

Challenge

In certain times of the year flower shops are overwhelmed and it is very difficult to manage orders for them. Long queues are formed at shops and if bouquets are not ordered in advance the result sometimes is not what clients are waiting for.

Solution

Design a flower shop app that allows users easily order and pick-up orders through the application and allows a high degree of customization whenever the user wants.

My role

UX designer designing Bloom's app from conception to delivery.

Date

January 2023 to April 2023

Process summary

Empathize

Deep understanding of the user. Who are the users? What are they expecting from the product? What are their pain points? What are their goals?

Define

Analyzing the data collected and the market. Concrete the issue to be solved.

Ideate

Exploring solutions for the issue to be solved and evaluate them.

Prototype

Turning ideas into something tangible to test on real users quickly, building scaled-down versions of the entire product or some specific features to test.

Test

The prototypes are tested by real users of the product. Users observations and interaction with prototypes are analyzed. Then changes are made according to their feedback.
